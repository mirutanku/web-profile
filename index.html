Hi, I'm Justin.

I'm a writer and communications professional previously based in Washington, D.C., and New York City, currently based in Cleveland, OH.

I spent the past decade writing and podcasting for The Ringer, covering a range of subjects: music, movies, television, video games, tech, politics, and current affairs. I was a Senior Staff Writer and the co-host of Sound Only.

Before that, I was an Account Director at Brunswick Group, a global communications firm. I worked extensively in crisis comms, reputation management, and public affairs.

I'm also a decidedly un-professional saxophonist and still less professional programmer. I'm developing Woodshed, a practice app for musicians who learn by ear.
